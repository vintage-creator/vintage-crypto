About

Staking is a great way to maximize your holdings by staking cryptocurrencies that would otherwise be sitting in your digital wallet. Once you have staked your assets, you can earn staking rewards on top of your holdings and grow them further by compounding those future rewards.

Vintage-crypto is a staking project officially licensed by Blockgames in partnership with Zuri Team that is leveraging blockchain technology to empower her users to earn "hooge" rewards while staking all or part of their tokens. Rewards are paid out in native token; $VIT weekly and users who do not claim their rewards as and when due will effectively lose them for that week. Currently, our native token $VIT is valued at 0.1ETH.
